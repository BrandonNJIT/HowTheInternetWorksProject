What is the internet? The internet is a vast network that connects computers all over the world.

Why do we use the internet? Its quite obvious that everyone now-a-days uses the internet A LOT. Many people use it for many reasons. For example, a business may use the internet for advertising and a website. A student may use the internet for schooling / classes. A kid may use the internet for video games. Someone can use the internet to even shop from home. There are almost endless uses for the internet and this is why we use it and why it has become such a crucial part of everyday life.
