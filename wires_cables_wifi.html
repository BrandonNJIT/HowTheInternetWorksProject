The ways of sending and receiving information on the internet

Electricity

Ethernet cable. Pros: fast, cheap. Cons: signal loss.

Light

Fiber optic cable. Pros: fast no signal loss. Cons: expensive, hard to work with.

Radio

Wireless. Pros: no cable. Con: signal loss, bad distance.

Extra Info

Bits are transferred between devices and use one or more of the three methods above to travel from sender to receiver.
